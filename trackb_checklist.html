► What to say when registering
Use the website form if one exists. If emailing, keep it short:

"Hi, I'm an active buyer looking for [property type] in [suburb/area]. Could you please add me to your mailing list for new listings? Happy to provide more detail on my requirements. Thanks."

Use the assigned nester's name and email. Don't over-explain. One paragraph max. The goal is just to get onto the list — the email poller handles everything after that.
